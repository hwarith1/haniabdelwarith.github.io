At Princeton, I have been privileged to serve as an Associate Instructor for courses in comparative politics. I have precepted:

  • POL 230 – Introduction to Comparative Politics (Prof. Andreas Wiedemann)
  • POL 366 – Politics in Africa (Prof. Jennifer Widner)
  • POL 379 – Politics in Southeast Asia (Prof. Nick Kuipers)

Throughout these experiences, I have sought to bring my regional expertise and lived experience in North Africa into the classroom. I excel as both a lecturer and a precept facilitator, creating spaces that are dynamic, informed, and inclusive.
